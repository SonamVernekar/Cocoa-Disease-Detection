About Our Project

We are dedicated to protecting cocoa crops with AI-powered disease detection.

Project Overview

Cocoa Disease Detection leverages cutting-edge machine learning technology to identify various diseases affecting cocoa plants, such as Black Pod Rot, Pod Borer, and more. With this tool, farmers can take immediate action to save their crops, reducing the risk of significant losses and ensuring sustainable cocoa production.

How It Works

Farmers log in and upload images for predictions.

Our platform allows farmers to easily log in and upload images of their crops or livestock. Using advanced prediction algorithms, we analyze the uploaded images to provide valuable recommendations tailored to their needs. Additionally, our translation feature ensures that language is no barrier to accessing important information.

  • Image Upload

    Farmers can upload images of their crops or livestock for analysis, making it easy to receive insights and recommendations.

  • Prediction Analysis

    Our system predicts potential issues with crops or livestock, helping farmers make informed decisions.

  • Personalized Recommendations

    Based on the analysis, we provide tailored recommendations to improve productivity and health.

  • Translation Services

    Our translation feature ensures that farmers can access information in their preferred language, making the platform more accessible.
